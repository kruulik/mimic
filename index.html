mimic

This game uses the Affectiva emotional recognition software to detect facial expressions.

In order to play you must use your webcam! Demo

How to play:
Emoji will start falling from the top of the screen. Try to match as many emoji as you can before time runs out. Match them by mimicing the expression with your face!

Tip: Turn your monitor brightness up and make sure there is ample light in the room.
